Cmb Numan 2

NOPQRSTUVWXYZ

abcdefghijklm

nopqrstuvwxyz

Christmas 1979 - all I wanted was either The Pleasure Principle by Gary Numan, or Replicas by Tubeway Army. My Dad bought me both! I was over the moon. One of the album inserts had the lyrics to all the songs written out in the most fascinating script. I spent hours practising the style. This font is probably nothing like the original after all these years, but I have named it accordingly!
